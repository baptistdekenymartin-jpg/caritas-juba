Safeguarding commitments

  • Zero tolerance Every safeguarding concern is documented, escalated, and reviewed within 24 hours.
  • Child protection All staff and volunteers refresh safeguarding and PSEA training every 12 months.
  • Partner due diligence We assess partner safeguarding practices before onboarding and monitor throughout projects.
  • Confidential reporting Anonymous channels are available for survivors and witnesses seeking protection.
