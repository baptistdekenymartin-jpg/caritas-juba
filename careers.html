Work with dignity and compassion

Caritas Archdiocese of Juba is an inclusive employer grounded in Catholic social teaching. We welcome professionals who uphold human dignity, stand with vulnerable communities, and bring integrity to every assignment.

Current Vacancies

Our teams accompany communities across South Sudan. When roles are open, they will be posted here with full job descriptions and duty station details.

There are no active recruitments at this time. Please check back soon or share your CV with hr@caritasjuba.org to be considered when opportunities arise.

Internships & Volunteering

We create pathways for students, graduates, and parish volunteers to gain hands-on experience in humanitarian response, peacebuilding, and community development.

  • Assignments tailored to your studies or areas of discernment.
  • Mentorship from program leads and parish partners.
  • Opportunities to serve across diocesan projects.

Email your CV, availability, and interests to hr@caritasjuba.org. Our HR team will reach out when suitable placements open.

How to Apply

  1. Submit a concise CV and a cover letter outlining your motivation and alignment with Caritas values.
  2. Email your application to hr@caritasjuba.org using the subject line: Position Title – Your Name.
  3. Attach documents in PDF format only. We do not request or accept facilitation fees at any stage.
  4. Shortlisted candidates will complete safeguarding declarations and reference checks before hire.

Caritas Archdiocese of Juba is an equal opportunity employer. We practice safeguarding and do not charge fees.
