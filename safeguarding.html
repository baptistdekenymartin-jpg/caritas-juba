Safeguarding: Protection from Abuse and Exploitation

Caritas Archdiocese of Juba is committed to a safe, respectful environment for children, young people, and vulnerable adults. We have zero tolerance for abuse, exploitation, harassment, or neglect by anyone representing us.

Our commitments

  • Prevent and respond to abuse and sexual exploitation and abuse (SEA).
  • Safe recruitment, mandatory training, and Codes of Conduct.
  • Confidential reporting channels; survivor-centred support; non-retaliation.
