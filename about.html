Who We Are

Caritas Archdiocese of Juba is the official organization and the humanitarian wing of the Catholic Archdiocese of Juba and part of Caritas South Sudan. It was created to carry out all social and humanitarian activities as it receives its mission from the Caritas South Sudan which is under the Catholic bishops of South Sudan.

Our Core Values

Caritas Archdiocese of Juba is committed to serve the human person in love and dignity, in solidarity with the poor and underprivileged. Our work is grounded in Catholic Social Teaching, which stresses the dignity of the human person regardless of culture, ethnicity, gender, or religion. This belief in the unity and diversity of humankind is the basic value we bring to what we do.

  • Respect for Human Dignity

    Recognizing the God-given dignity of each person we serve.

  • Transparency & Accountability

    Maximizing responsible stewardship of all resources entrusted to us.

  • Solidarity with the Poor

    Standing with the poor at all times and places.

  • Social Justice

    A constant concern for justice and the common good.

  • Spirit of Partnership

    Endeavoring to promote and retain partnerships with like-minded organizations.
