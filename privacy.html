Privacy

Your Data, Protected with Care

We only collect the information we need to accompany communities, communicate transparently, and keep people safe.

Purpose limited Secure storage Respectful consent

At a glance

  • Minimal data: Contact details and service analytics only.
  • No selling: We never trade or rent personal data.
  • Rights honoured: Access, correction, and deletion on request.

Questions? We respond to privacy requests within 30 calendar days.

Information we collect

Details you share when subscribing, submitting enquiries, or raising safeguarding alerts.

  • Names and contact information
  • Messages and feedback you provide
  • Optional demographic insights for programme design

How we use data

We rely on data to answer your questions, share updates you opt into, and strengthen digital security.

  • Response and follow-up communications
  • Safeguarding case documentation
  • Anonymous analytics to improve online services

When we share data

Sharing is limited to trusted service providers or when required by law to protect people from harm.

  • Encrypted email and cloud services
  • Legal authorities if safety is at risk
  • Global Caritas partners under data agreements

Your data rights

You control how we use your information. Contact us any time to exercise your rights or ask for support.

Request assistance

  • Email: info@caritasjuba.org (privacy focal point)
  • Subject line: Privacy Request – Your Name
  • Include: The request type (access, update, delete) and any relevant details.

We will verify your identity before sharing or changing personal data.

Our response timeline

  1. We acknowledge your request within five working days.
  2. We assess scope and confirm any supporting documents needed.
  3. We deliver a response or update within 30 calendar days.

Data security & retention

Safeguards are embedded in our systems, contracts, and daily operations.

  • Encrypted storage with role-based access controls
  • Retention schedules aligned with programme and legal needs
  • Secure disposal of paper and digital records
  • Regular reviews of third-party processors

Need to withdraw consent?

Email communications@caritasjuba.org to unsubscribe from newsletters or alerts.

Last updated: 30 October 2025. We will refresh this notice when practices change.
